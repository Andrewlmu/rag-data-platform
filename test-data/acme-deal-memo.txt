CONFIDENTIAL - DEAL MEMORANDUM

Company: ACME Manufacturing Corp.
Deal Type: Leveraged Buyout (LBO)
Date: November 1, 2025
Fund: Titan Capital Partners III

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Titan Capital Partners proposes to acquire 100% of ACME Manufacturing Corp for an enterprise value of $450 million. ACME is a leading manufacturer of industrial components with strong market position and consistent cash flows.

KEY METRICS
- Enterprise Value: $450M
- Equity Investment: $180M (40%)
- Debt Financing: $270M (60%)
- Revenue (LTM): $320M
- EBITDA (LTM): $65M
- Entry Multiple: 6.9x EBITDA
- Projected IRR: 23-27%
- Holding Period: 5 years

INVESTMENT THESIS
1. Market leader in industrial automation components (32% market share)
2. Recurring revenue model (60% of sales from maintenance contracts)
3. Strong operational improvement opportunities
4. Roll-up strategy: acquire 3-4 smaller competitors
5. International expansion into Asian markets

FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS
Year 1: Revenue $340M, EBITDA $72M (22.4% margin)
Year 2: Revenue $385M, EBITDA $85M (22.1% margin)
Year 3: Revenue $440M, EBITDA $101M (23.0% margin)
Year 4: Revenue $510M, EBITDA $122M (23.9% margin)
Year 5: Revenue $595M, EBITDA $149M (25.0% margin)

EXIT STRATEGY
Target exit multiple: 8.5x EBITDA
Projected exit value: $1,267M (at Year 5 EBITDA)
Expected return: 3.5x MOIC, 28% IRR

RISKS
- Customer concentration (top 3 customers = 45% revenue)
- Competitive pressure from Chinese manufacturers
- Cyclical industry exposure
- Key person risk (CEO succession)

TEAM
CEO: Michael Chen (15 years experience)
CFO: Sarah Williams (PE background, ex-McKinsey)
COO: James Rodriguez (operational excellence, Lean Six Sigma)
